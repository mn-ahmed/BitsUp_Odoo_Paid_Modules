Quickly file GST - Returns and Invoices via JSON

The Indian Accounting follows the GST (Goods Services Tax), hence filing it is really necessary for every business. While filing GSTR, either you need to fill the form manually or upload the JSON of details directly.

Time is precious so you need to save it to file your GSTR in quick and easy steps. Hence, uploading JSON to file GSTR can help you to make the process quick and easy.

As JSON is not a human-readable document report. Hence, you also need a human-readable report of your invoices to track the parts of your business.

Prepare Invoice Reports in Odoo for the Ease of FIling GST

Odoo has solutions for most of your business-related problems. Moreover, it has one of the most promising accounting solutions. To enhance the Indian Accounting Solution we introduced Odoo GST - Returns and Invoices.

GST - Returns and Invoices authorizes you to generate the JSON format of the invoices to file the GSTR1 as well as GSTR2. Not just JSON, but you can also generate CSV for your report so that tracking become easy.

Track the HSN Summary of the Odoo Invoices

Now, you can easily track the GST applied to every product with the help of the module Odoo GST - Returns and Invoices. The Odoo app permits you to print the HSN summary in a human-readable format.

You can get the Tax HSN summary from invoices as well as invoice reports. Moreover, you can check them from GST Invoices in JSON as well as CSV format.

It authorizes you to know all your tax deductions and makes the process transparent for you. It aids you to classify goods from all over the world in a systematic and logical manner. This brings in a systematic classification of goods and facilitates international trade as well.
